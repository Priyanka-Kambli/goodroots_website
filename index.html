How to Use

Simple steps to transform your hair care routine

1

Mix

Take 2-3 tablespoons of powder, add water or rose water to make a smooth paste

2

Apply

Apply evenly on damp hair and scalp. Massage gently for 2-3 minutes

3

Rest

Leave for 15-20 minutes. For deep treatment, cover with a shower cap

4

Rinse

Rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water. No need for conditioner

Recommended: Use 1-2 times weekly for best results

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about GoodRoots

How often should I use this hair mask?

For best results, use 1-2 times per week. If you have oily hair, you can use it up to 3 times a week. For dry hair, once a week is sufficient.

Can it be used on colored or chemically treated hair?

Yes! Our Ayurvedic hair mask is completely natural and doesn't contain any harsh chemicals that would strip color. Many customers with colored hair report that it actually helps maintain their color longer.

Is it suitable for all hair types?

Absolutely. The blend works beautifully on straight, wavy, curly, and coily hair types. It helps balance oil production for oily scalps while providing needed moisture for dry hair.

How long does one packet last?

One 100g packet provides approximately 6-8 applications, depending on your hair length and thickness. For shoulder-length hair, you can expect about 7 uses per packet.

What is the shelf life?

The powder has a shelf life of 24 months from the date of manufacture when stored in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
